About LIXR

Welcome to an era where everything is aided by technology and why should night life be left behind.

LIXR is the latest solution for all those party peeps who have been longing for an application to get everything they want to know about the parties, events, food and signature drinks. Now, get the whole lot about the best hangout places, waterholes, and more by just downloading the app, LIXR.

A free, crowd sourced community, LIXR redefines your nightlife experience. It helps you avail big discounts; connect with like-minded party goers, establishment owners, and receive real -time updates like a trusted guide.

LIXR is a favourite with pub owners, too. They can promote their signature cocktails and best combo offer deals.
